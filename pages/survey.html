Final Verdict
80.8%

chose the human-designed brand

The human design won on visual quality and emotional impact. AI scored competitively on brand coherence — but when it came to making people feel something, the gap was decisive: 4.5 vs 2.9 out of 5. Even those who couldn't detect the AI (31%) still preferred the human work.
