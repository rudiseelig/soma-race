001 — Introduction

Can a machine
out-design a human?

Both received the same brief: create a complete brand identity for SOMA, a modern aphrodisiac oil. One is a human designer working through research, intuition, and craft. The other is an artificial intelligence processing data, patterns, and probabilities.

This website documents their parallel journeys — from the same starting point to two very different destinations. Explore the process, compare the results, and decide for yourself.

Human Designer

Research-driven, emotionally guided, handcrafted through iteration and intuition.

AI Designer

Data-informed, pattern-matched, generated through prompts and parameters.
